Fun Facts

I am adopted

I was adopted officially back in 2010 a few weeks before my fifth birthday. I was put into the foster system when I was two but was still in contact with my biological family up untill I was four. I have one biological sister and five other siblings that I gained from being adopted. I love being adopted. It has given me lots of opportunities and helped me in the long run. I get to have two families now and the support I recieve is unmatched.
