Fun Facts

I am adopted

I was adopted officially back in 2010 a few weeks before my fifth birthday. I was put into the foster system when I was two but was still in contact with my biological family up untill I was four. I have one biological sister and five other siblings that I gained from being adopted. I love being adopted. It has given me lots of opportunities and helped me in the long run. I get to have two families now and the support I recieve is unmatched.

Niches

I've got a lot of little niches that I've picked up over the years. Such things include crocheting, archery, piano, writing, sewing, paint, draw, and others that I cannot think of off the top of my head. The point is I can do a lot of things and could be called a jack of all trades kind of gal.

Surrounded by the military

I am completely surrounded by military life. I was born with family memebers being in the military and when I was adopted my father was in the army. He was in for 28 years. Then I almost joined the navy before I recieved a full scholarship to the University of North Alabama (UNA). But then my boyfriend joined the marines so I may never escape the military lifestye for good.
